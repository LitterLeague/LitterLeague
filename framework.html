Litter League

In 2024, with the aim to inspire more youth to step up on reducing landfills, Litter League is curating a 7-part video series under the CONTROL framework as part of its educational outreach program Through the 7 steps of Care, Observe, Navigate, Target, Resolve, Organise and Lead, each youth can be empowered and mobilized to create an impact by making a notable contribution in the reduction of landfill
