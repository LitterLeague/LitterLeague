Welcome to Litter League

Litter League is an anti-littering club in Hyderabad, India that aims to promote waste reduction and solid waste management practices. The club plays a crucial role in helping to create a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable community for everyone.


About the Founder

Nidhi Reddy

Hi! I am Nidhi Reddy Gangumalla, a high school student and an environmental enthusiast. My motto is to learn by doing. I strongly believe that all learning should lead to taking action in the real world. All of us have learnt from a young age about how we should throw waste in dustbins, avoid littering and the various consequences of global warming and climate change. However, I felt that this learning has been unable to take action in the world. The problem of littering still exists despite the awareness trying to be built in people. I have created this club to encourage people to take small actions that could bring together a significant impact.


Vision & Mission

Vision:

At Litter League, our vision is to create a sustainable world where waste is minimized, resources are conserved, and communities thrive in harmony with the environment. We envision a future where every individual and business is committed to responsible waste management practices, leading to a cleaner, healthier planet for generations to come.

Mission:

Our mission at Litter League is to promote environmental stewardship and facilitate the transition to a circular economy by providing innovative recycling solutions, raising awareness about the importance of waste reduction, and fostering partnerships with local communities, businesses, and government agencies. Through education, advocacy, and action, we strive to empower individuals and organizations to embrace sustainable practices, reduce their environmental footprint, and contribute to a more resilient and prosperous planet.
